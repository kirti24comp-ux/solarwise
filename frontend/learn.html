Key Components 🔧

🛸🔋

Solar Panels

Capture sunlight and convert to DC electricity

Fun Fact: A typical panel produces 250-400 watts!
⚡🔄

Inverter

Converts DC to AC power for your home

Fun Fact: Inverters are 95-98% efficient!
🔋⚡

Battery Storage

Store excess energy for nighttime use

Fun Fact: Lithium-ion batteries last 10-15 years!
📱📊

Monitoring System

Track your solar production in real-time

Fun Fact: Monitor from your phone!

System Types ⚡

🏠⚡

Grid-Tied

Connected to utility grid, no battery needed

  • ✅ Lowest cost
  • ✅ Net metering benefits
  • ❌ No power during outage
🏠🔋⚡

Hybrid

Grid-connected with battery backup

  • ✅ Best of both worlds
  • ✅ Power during outages
  • 💰 Higher initial cost
🏔️🔋

Off-Grid

Complete independence from utility

  • ✅ Energy independence
  • ✅ Perfect for remote areas
  • 💰 Highest initial cost
